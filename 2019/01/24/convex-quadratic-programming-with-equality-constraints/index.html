等式约束凸二次规划的Lagrange解法

Posted on 2019年1月24日2019年9月18日by jasonpeng

leetcode 数据库题目全部题解

Table of Contents

  • 问题形式
  • 问题条件
  • 求解过程

问题形式

要解决这样形式的二次规划问题：
\begin{align}
min \quad \frac{1}{2}x^{T}Hx+c^{T}x \\
s.t. \quad Ax = b
\end{align}

问题条件

\( H \subset R^{n \times n} \)对称正定；
\( A \subset R^{m \times n} \)行满秩；
\( c,x \subset R^n \);
\( b \subset R^m \)

对称正定；
\( A \subset R^{m \times n} \)行满秩；
\( c,x \subset R^n \);
\( b \subset R^m \)

求解过程

用Lagrange乘子法来解，首先定义Lagrange函数 \begin{align} \label{lagrange_function} L(x, \lambda) \quad = \quad \frac{1}{2}x^{T}Hx+c^{T}x - \lambda ^T (Ax-b) \end{align} 由可微函数一阶必要性可得: \begin{align} \nabla_xL(x,\lambda) &= \quad 0 \\ \nabla_{\lambda}L(x,\lambda) &= \quad 0 \label{necessity} \end{align}

其中:
\begin{align}\label{necessity_step3}
\nabla_xL(x,\lambda) \quad = \quad \frac{1}{2} \nabla_x(x^THx)
+\nabla_x(c^Tx)-\nabla_x(\lambda ^T(Ax-b))
\end{align}
先计算\( \frac{1}{2} \nabla_x(x^THx) \)
\begin{align}\label{necessity_step3_part1}
\frac{1}{2} \nabla_x(x^THx) \quad = \quad \frac{1}{2}
\begin{bmatrix}
\dfrac{\partial{(x^THx)}}{\partial{x_1}},
\dfrac{\partial{(x^THx)}}{\partial{x_2}},
\dots,
\dfrac{\partial{(x^THx)}}{\partial{x_i}},
\dots,
\dfrac{\partial{(x^THx)}}{\partial{x_n}}
\end{bmatrix}^T
\end{align}
记\( H=\begin{bmatrix} h_1,h_2,\dots,h_i,\dots,h_n \end{bmatrix}^T \)
\begin{align}\label{necessity_step3_part2}
\dfrac{\partial{(x^THx)}}{\partial{x_i}} \quad &= \quad \dfrac{\partial{\left[ (x_1h_1+x_2h_2+\dots+x_ih_i+\dots+x_nh_n)x\right]}}{\partial{x_i}} \\ \quad &=\quad \dfrac{\partial(x_1h_1x)+\partial(x_2h_2x)+\dots+\partial(x_ih_ix)+\dots+\partial(x_nh_nx)}{\partial{x_i}}
\end{align}
其中:
\begin{align}\label{necessity_step3_part3}
\dfrac{\partial(x_jh_jx)}{\partial{x_i}} \quad = \quad x_jh_{ji} \quad (j \neq i) \\
\dfrac{\partial(x_ih_ix)}{\partial{x_i}} \quad = \quad h_{i1}x_1+h_{i2}x_2+\dots 2h_{ii}x_i+\dots+h_{in}x_n
\end{align}
将\eqref{necessity_step3_part3}代入\eqref{necessity_step3_part2}中可得:
\begin{align}\label{necessity_step3_part4}
\dfrac{\partial{(x^THx)}}{\partial{x_i}} \quad = \quad x_1h_{1i}+x_2h_{2i}+\dots+(h_{i1}x_1+h_{i2}x_2+\dots \\ 2h_{ii}x_i+\dots+h_{in}x_n)+\dots+x_nh_{ni}
\end{align}
注意:\( h_{i1}=h_{1i} \)，则有
\begin{align}\label{necessity_step3_part5}
\dfrac{\partial{(x^THx)}}{\partial{x_i}} \quad &= \quad 2(h_{i1}x_1+h_{i2}x_2+\dots h_{ii}x_i+\dots+h_{in}x_n) \\
\quad &= \quad 2h_ix
\end{align}
由上面的值，更新\eqref{necessity_step3_part1}：
\begin{align}\label{necessity_step3_part6}
\frac{1}{2} \nabla_x(x^THx) \quad &=\quad \frac{1}{2}
\begin{bmatrix}
2h_1x,
2h_2x,
\dots,
2h_ix,
\dots,
2h_nx
\end{bmatrix}^T \\
&= \quad
\begin{bmatrix}
h_1x,
h_2x,
\dots,
h_ix,
\dots,
h_nx
\end{bmatrix}^T \\
&= \quad
\begin{bmatrix}
h_{11}x_1+h_{12}x_2+\dots+h_{1i}x_i+\dots+h_{1n}x_n \\
h_{21}x_1+h_{22}x_2+\dots+h_{2i}x_i+\dots+h_{2n}x_n \\
\vdots \\
h_{i1}x_1+h_{i2}x_2+\dots+h_{ii}x_i+\dots+h_{in}x_n \\
\vdots \\
h_{n1}x_1+h_{n2}x_2+\dots+h_{ni}x_i+\dots+h_{nn}x_n
\end{bmatrix} \\
&= \quad Hx
\end{align}
即\( \frac{1}{2} \nabla_x(x^THx)=Hx \) 
\begin{align}\label{necessity_step3_part7}
\nabla_x(c^Tx) \quad &= \quad
\begin{bmatrix}
\dfrac{\partial(c^Tx)}{\partial x_1},\dfrac{\partial(c^Tx)}{\partial x_2},\dots,\dfrac{\partial(c^Tx)}{\partial x_n}
\end{bmatrix}^T \\
\quad &= \quad
\begin{bmatrix}
c_1,c_2,\dots,c_n
\end{bmatrix}^T \\
\quad &= \quad c
\end{align}
同理可得:\( \nabla_x(\lambda ^T(Ax-b)) \quad = \quad \nabla_x(\lambda^TAx) = \quad A^T \lambda \) 
可得：
\begin{equation}\label{necessity_step3_part10}
\nabla_xL(x,\lambda) \quad = \quad Hx+c-A^T \lambda \quad = 0
\end{equation}
同理可得:
\begin{align}\label{necessity_step3_part8}
\nabla_{\lambda}L(x,\lambda) \quad =\quad
\dfrac{1}{2} \nabla_{\lambda}(x^THx) + \nabla_{\lambda}(c^Tx)-\nabla_{\lambda}(\lambda^T(Ax-b)) =
- \nabla_{\lambda}[ \lambda^T(Ax-b) ]
\end{align}
记录:\( K=Ax- b=[k_1,k_2,\dots,k_i,\dots,k_m]^T  \)
\begin{align}\label{necessity_step3_part9}
\nabla_{\lambda}[ \lambda^T(Ax-b)]\quad &= \quad
\nabla_{\lambda}[\lambda^T K]\\ \quad &= \quad [\dfrac{\partial(\lambda^TK)}{\partial\lambda_1},\dfrac{\partial(\lambda^TK)}{\partial\lambda_2},\dots,\dfrac{\partial(\lambda^TK)}{\partial\lambda_i},\dots,\dfrac{\partial(\lambda^TK)}{\partial\lambda_m}] \\ \quad &=
\quad [k_1,k_2,\dots,k_i,\dots,k_m]^T \\ \quad &= \quad K
\end{align}
因此可得:
\begin{equation}\label{necessity_step3_part11}
\nabla_{\lambda}L(x,\lambda) \quad = \quad -K \\ \quad = \quad -(Ax-b) \\ \quad = \quad 0
\end{equation}
由\eqref{necessity_step3_part10},\eqref{necessity_step3_part11}可得:
整理后可得:
\begin{align}\label{necessity_step2}
Hx-A^T \lambda= -c \\
-Ax=-b
\end{align}
写成矩阵形式:
\begin{align}\label{necessity_matrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
H & -A^T \\
-A & 0
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
x \\
\lambda
\end{bmatrix}
\quad = \quad
\begin{bmatrix}
-c \\
-b
\end{bmatrix}
\end{align}
因此只要矩阵P可逆，就能得到解析解:
\begin{equation}\label{necessity_step4_part1}
P \quad = \quad
\begin{bmatrix}
H & -A^T \\
-A & 0
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation}
幸运的是，在问题条件下，有定理可以保证矩阵\(P \)可逆(非奇异)。计算\(P\)的逆矩阵得到解。
不妨设\(P\)的逆矩阵如下：
\begin{equation}\label{necessity_step4_part2}
P^{-1} \quad = \quad
\begin{bmatrix}
H & -A^T \\
-A & 0
\end{bmatrix}^{-1}
\quad = \quad
\begin{bmatrix}
G & -B^T \\
-B & C
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation}
由等式：
\begin{equation}\label{necessity_step4_part3}
PP^{-1} \quad = \quad
\begin{bmatrix}
H & -A^T \\
-A & 0
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
G & -B^T \\
-B & C
\end{bmatrix}
\quad =\quad I
\end{equation}
联立方程可得解:
\begin{align}\label{necessity_step4_part4}
G &= H^{-1}-H^{-1}A^T(AH^{-1}A^T)^{-1}AH^{-1} \\
B &= (AH^{-1}A^T)^{-1}AH^{-1} \\
C &= -(AH^{-1}A^T)^{-1}
\end{align}
\begin{align}\label{necessity_step4_part5}
\begin{bmatrix}
\overline{x} \\
\overline{\lambda}
\end{bmatrix}
\quad = \quad
\begin{bmatrix}
G & -B^T \\
-B & C
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
-c \\
-b
\end{bmatrix}
\quad = \quad
\begin{bmatrix}
-Gc+B^Tb \\
Bc-Cb
\end{bmatrix}
\end{align}
如此就可编写程序，求解。

python代码:

# -*- coding: utf-8 -*-
"""
Created on Thu Dec 13 21:55:50 2018
@author: pasion
"""

import numpy as np
import numpy.linalg as npla

def cqpec_lagrange_multiplier(H,A,c,b):
    '''Convex Quadratic programming with Equality constraints'''
    '''
     min 0.5x'Hx+c'x
     s.t. Ax=b
     
     H (n x n) is symmetric, real, nonsingular and positive definite. 
     A (m x n), the row must be full rank.
     c,x (n x 1). b (m x 1).
    '''
    #the inv function can check that H is symmetric, nosingular, positive definite or not.
    invH = npla.inv(H)
    #if the row of A lacks rank (<m), part1 will be Singular
    part1=npla.multi_dot([A,invH,A.T])
    #print 'part1 dim shape',part1.ndim,part1.shape
    invPart1=npla.inv(part1)
    B=npla.multi_dot([invPart1,A,invH])
    part2=npla.multi_dot([invH,A.T,B])
    G=invH-part2
    C=-invPart1
    x=np.dot(B.T,b)-np.dot(G,c)
    lmbd=np.dot(B,c)-np.dot(C,b)
    val2=npla.multi_dot([x.T,H,x])
    val0=np.multiply(0.5,val2)
    val1=np.dot(c.T,x)
    val=val0+val1
    return x,lmbd,val

def test1():
    H=np.array([[3,-1,0],[-1,2,-1],[0,-1,1]]).reshape(3,3)
    print 'H dim shape',H.ndim,H.shape
    c=np.array([1,1,1]).reshape(3,1)
    print 'c dim shape',c.ndim,c.shape
    A=np.array([1,2,1]).reshape(1,3)
    print 'A dim shape',A.ndim,A.shape
    b=np.array([4]).reshape(1,1)
    print 'b dim shape',b.ndim,b.shape
    x,lmbd,value=cqpec_lagrange_multiplier(H,A,c,b)
    print 'x',x
    print 'lmbd',lmbd
    print 'value',value

def test2():
    H=np.array([[2,-2,0],[-2,4,0],[0,0,2]]).reshape(3,3)
    print 'H dim shape',H.ndim,H.shape
    c=np.array([0,0,1]).reshape(3,1)
    print 'c dim shape',c.ndim,c.shape
    A=np.array([1,1,1,2,-1,1]).reshape(2,3)
    print 'A dim shape',A.ndim,A.shape
    b=np.array([4,2]).reshape(2,1)
    print 'b dim shape',b.ndim,b.shape
    x,lmbd,value=cqpec_lagrange_multiplier(H,A,c,b)
    print 'x',x
    print 'lmbd',lmbd
    print 'value',value

test1()
test2()
